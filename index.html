Guenda Palmirotta

Postdoctoral researcher at the Universität Paderborn, Institute of Mathematics and a team member of the Spektralanalysis group.

Research interests

  • Harmonic analysis on non-compact symmetric spaces $G/K$
  • Spectral analysis on locally symmetric spaces and buildings
  • Numerical investigation of $n$-discs scattering systems and Schottky surfaces
