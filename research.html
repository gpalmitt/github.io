Research

"What is mathematics? It is only a systematic effort of solving puzzles posed by nature." (Shakuntala Devi)

Research interest:

  • Harmonic analysis on non-compact symmetric spaces $G/K$
  • Analysis on invariant differential operators
  • Solvability of (systems of) linear partial differential equations
  • Fourier transforms and Paley-Wiener-(Schwartz) theorems for sections of (in)homogeneous vector bundles on $G/K$
  • Quantum chaos/ergodicity: Patterson-Sullivan distributions, Pollicott-Ruelle and quantum resonances, ...
  • Dispersive and Strichartz estimates
  • Numerical analysis
  • Applications to physics
