    • Mathematical Careers Day

    Mathematicians are among the most sought-after professionals in Luxembourg. But what kind of jobs do mathematicians really do? In order to show to our students the large possibilites of a mathematical career, Prof. Gabor Wiese, Luca Notarnicola and myself, we create the Mathematical Careers Day. I was co-organizer of the two first editions and created the first draft of the Mathematical Careers Brochure.

    • 2023: Entering the job market after your mathematical studies, FTSM Newsletter.
    • 2019: Mathematical Careers in Luxembourg and beyond - Brochure (first edition).
    • 2019: Another success story for the Mathematical Careers Day, FTSM Newsletter.
    • 2018: Mathematical Careers Day 2018: great success for the first edition, FTSM Newsletter.

    Luxembourg United

    Luxembourg United is a football robot team founded in 2016 by Patrice Caire. In 2017, I joined the team and helped them to "train" the robots to play soccer. Our goal at the time was to participate in the RopoCup 2017 robot soccer competition in Japan.
