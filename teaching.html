Math Boot Camp - University of Luxembourg

"Making math easier, one scoop at a time" is the moto of this Math Boot Camp at the University of Luxembourg. The camp offers a mathematical warm up to all first year bachelor students to ease the transition between high school and university, and is arranged in two independent weeks.

  • September 4-8, 2023, Belval (Luxembourg): Leader of the Math BootCamp, 4th edition of the Mathematical Camp Week.
  • September 2022, Belval (Luxembourg): Lecturer for the first week Math BootCamp.
  • September 2019, Belval (Luxembourg): Lecturer for the second week Math PrepCamp course "Nombres complexes".
